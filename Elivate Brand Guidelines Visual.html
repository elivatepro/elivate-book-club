Brand Guidelines

Elivate represents the elevation of thought, culture, and human potential through reading. These guidelines ensure consistency across all touchpoints while maintaining our commitment to literary excellence and cultural integrity.

Core Identity

Mission: Build a global literary culture rooted in curiosity, integrity, and meaningful conversation.

Vision: A world where reading communities shape culture instead of reacting to it.

Typography

Our typographic system balances literary tradition with digital clarity, ensuring optimal readability across all contexts.

Display Type — Playfair Display

The Elevation of Thought

Used for headlines, book titles, and hero text. Weights: 400 / 700 / 900.

Body Type — Crimson Pro

In a world saturated with algorithmic recommendations and commercial book lists, Elivate emerges as a corrective force—a space where reading is treated as a serious, life-shaping endeavor.

Optimized for long-form reading. Weights: 400 / 500 / 600.

Voice & Tone

Our voice is intelligent, direct, and culturally grounded—serious without being rigid, precise without unnecessary ornamentation.

Messaging Principles

✓ Do

Clear and Direct: "Join a community of serious readers."

Intellectually Honest: "We select books that challenge and expand thinking."

Culturally Aware: "Literature shapes culture. We shape how literature is read."

✗ Don't

Hyperbolic: "The most amazing book club ever!"

Condescending: "For those smart enough to appreciate real literature."

Marketing Speak: "Optimize your reading journey with our AI-powered recommendations!"
