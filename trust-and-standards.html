Editorial Standards

Integrity at every step.

Elivate operates with complete editorial independence. We never accept payment for coverage, and every book earns its selection through merit alone.

No Pay-to-Play

Publishers, authors, and agents cannot buy placement.

Transparency

Selection criteria and meeting summaries available to Patrons.

Diverse Perspectives

Board composition ensures varied backgrounds and viewpoints.

Community Guidelines

  • Engage thoughtfully with text and members.
  • Debate ideas, never individuals.
  • Professional tone; no spam or promotion.
  • No off-topic proselytizing.
  • No plagiarism; respect copyrights.

Moderation Process

  1. First violation → warning.
  2. Second → 7-day suspension.
  3. Third → permanent removal.
  4. Appeals via community@elivatebookclub.com.

Privacy & Data

  • We collect only essential info (name, email, preferences).
  • No sale or rental of member data.
  • Opt out of non-essential communication anytime.
  • Export or delete your data on request.

Your Rights

  • Access and review data stored.
  • Request deletion.
  • Download discussion history.
  • Opt out of analytics cookies.

Accessibility

Elivate targets WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. Every image receives alt text, keyboard navigation is mandatory, and screen reader semantics are audited quarterly.
