How We Select Books
Our Editorial Process
Each month, our editorial board reviews between 150-250 books for consideration. The selection process follows these steps:

Initial Submission Review - All submitted books receive a first reading by at least two board members
Long List Creation - Approximately 20 books advance to full board consideration
Deep Reading Phase - Board members fully read long-list selections
Discussion and Voting - The board meets to discuss merits and select the final choice
Author Notification - Selected authors are contacted 60 days before their feature month

Selection Criteria:

Literary quality and craftsmanship
Relevance to contemporary issues or enduring themes
Potential for generating meaningful discussion
Diversity of perspectives and voices
Accessibility to general educated readers

Trust & Standards Page
Our Editorial Standards
How We Maintain Quality
Elivate Book Club operates with complete editorial independence. Our selection process is designed to identify exceptional books while avoiding commercial influence.
No Pay-to-Play
We never accept payment for book selection. Publishers, authors, and agents cannot buy placement. All books earn their selection through merit alone.
Transparent Process
Our editorial board's selection criteria are public. Meeting minutes (with sensitive information redacted) are available to Patron members. We publish an annual report detailing our selection statistics and process improvements.
Diverse Perspectives
We actively seek books from diverse authors and publishers. Our board includes members from different backgrounds, ensuring varied perspectives in our selection process.
