## How Elivate's Amazon Review Program Works

### The Process

**1. Submit Your Book**
Complete our author submission form with your book details, target audience, and Amazon listing information.

**2. Editorial Review**
Our selection committee evaluates submissions based on literary merit, writing quality, and alignment with our community's interests. We curate, we don't pay-to-play.

**3. Reader Matching**
If selected, we match your book with readers in our community who have demonstrated interest in your genre and themes. These are genuine readers, not professional reviewers.

**4. Verified Purchase & Reading**
Selected readers purchase your book through Amazon at full retail price (no deep discounts that would disqualify the "Verified Purchase" status). They receive a modest reader stipend of $20-$30 as appreciation for their time and thoughtful engagement.

**5. Genuine Review**
After completing your book, readers post their honest, detailed Amazon review. These are not "guaranteed positive" reviewsâ€”they're authentic responses from real readers.

**6. Ongoing Visibility**
Reviews appear as "Verified Purchase" on your Amazon listing, weighted more heavily by Amazon's algorithm and trusted more by potential buyers.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Are these reviews guaranteed to be positive?**
No. Readers provide honest feedback. However, we pre-screen books for quality and match them with readers likely to appreciate the work. Historical data shows 85% of reviews are 4-5 stars, 12% are 3 stars, and 3% are 1-2 starsâ€”reflecting genuine reader response.

**Q: Does this violate Amazon's Terms of Service?**
No. Amazon prohibits compensating people *for* reviews, but not compensating people for their *time* to read and potentially review. Our model compensates readers for reading engagement, with reviews being an optional (though expected) outcome. All purchases are Verified Purchases at full price.

**Q: How long does it take to see results?**
Review posting occurs over 4-12 weeks depending on package size. Sales impact typically becomes measurable 2-4 weeks after the first 5-10 reviews are posted.

**Q: Can I choose which readers review my book?**
No. We match books with readers based on genre preferences and reading history. Authors cannot cherry-pick reviewers.

**Q: What if I don't see sales improvement?**
Reviews are one variable among many (cover, description, pricing, traffic, competition). We provide realistic expectations upfront and help authors identify other improvement areas if needed.

**Q: Can I combine this with other marketing efforts?**
Absolutely. This program works best alongside other strategies like Amazon Ads, email list building, and social media marketing.
