FAQs

Straight answers about compliance and results.

Are reviews guaranteed to be positive?

No. We encourage honest feedback. We pre-screen for quality and match appreciative readers; historical data shows ~85% 4-5 stars.

Does this violate Amazon TOS?

No. Readers buy at full price for Verified Purchase, stipends are for time, and we never request positive ratings. All language is compliance-checked.

How fast do reviews post?

4-12 weeks depending on package size. Effects on conversion typically appear after the first 5-10 reviews.

Can I pick the readers?

No. We match based on genre interest, history, and account health to keep results authentic and compliant.

What if results are weak?

Reviews amplify strong fundamentals. We’ll flag issues (cover, description, pricing) and can advise on optimizations.
